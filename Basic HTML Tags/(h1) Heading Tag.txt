#HEADING TAG

-Heading tag is used to mark Headings in HTML from (h1) to (h6), we have
 tags for the most important to the least least important heading.
Ex:-     
           <h1> Most Importent Heading <h1> 
              <h2> Another Heading <h2>
              <h3> Another Heading <h3>
              <h4> Another Heading <h4>
              <h5> Another Heading <h5>
              <h6> Another Heading <h6>
