#RIGHT TO LEFT LANGUAGE WEBSITE

-Same time we creat website for language it's write right to left like urdu or arbic
 for this type of website.
-For writen website right to left use dir="rtl", the rtl for right to right and ltr
 for left to right. 
-use this dir in html tag.

Ex:-
      <!DOCTYPE html>
<html dir="rtl">
<head>
    <title>Right to Left</title>
</head>
<body>
    <h1>right to Left</h1>
</body>
</html>
